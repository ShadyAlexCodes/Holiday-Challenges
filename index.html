The Concept

When the Holiday's come around families across the world are often purchasing Holiday Advent Calendars. This project brings the calendar to you, and introduces holiday activities for all ages! From simple coding questions all the way to facts about computer science.

The Building Process

The inspiration for this idea came from a neighbor who had just bought one for themselves, and during that time I was looking at other projects such as this one. After much research, and feedback on the idea I began developing it. I wanted to create something cute, and fun to represent my questions.
